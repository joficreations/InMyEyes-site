Privacy Policy

Jofi Creations AB  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how the InMyEyes app handles your data.

What information is collected?

InMyEyes does not collect or store personal data. No account is required.

When you use the app, the photo you have selected is sent to Anthropic's API (Claude) to generate text. The image is sent solely for this purpose and is not stored by us. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for how they handle data.

Photos and camera access

The app requests access to your photo library and/or camera only so that you can choose or take a photo to use in the app. We do not store any images on our servers.

Purchases and subscriptions

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Analytics

We do not collect anonymised usage data or analytics beyond what Apple may collect via the App Store (see Apple's Privacy Policy).

Third-party services

  • Anthropic – image processing for text generation. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store – distribution and purchase handling. Apple Privacy Policy

Children

The app is designed for the whole family and may be used by children. The app does not collect personal data and requires no account. Photos sent for text generation are processed by Anthropic — we recommend that parents review Anthropic's Privacy Policy. In-app purchases require Apple ID authorisation and can be protected with a password or Face ID in the device settings.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date of the most recent update is always shown at the top of this page.
